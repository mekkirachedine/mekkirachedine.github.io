Finger Injuries

Description: Can occur when someone is cooking or playing sports.

Symptoms
  • Severe pain or deformity
  • Uncontrolled bleeding
  • Exposed bones or tendons
  • Finger loss of sensation
  • Finger is pale or bluish in color
Instructions
  1. Clean the cut.
  2. Note if the blood squirts out or oozes out.
  3. Check how deep the wound is.
  4. Stop the bleeding.
  5. Put an antibiotic cream or ointment on the cut.
  6. Bandage the cut.
